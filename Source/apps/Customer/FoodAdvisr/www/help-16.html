Simply toggle 'on' any allergens you wish to avoid.

Any changes you make to your allergens are automatically saved and you can see your updated scan results by pressing the 'Results' button at the top right of the app.

Any items selected that contain your selected allergens will appear with a black sqaure marked with a white 'A' and will be greyed out.

Tap on the sqaure to see which allergen the item contains.

Any items selected that are marked as 'may contain' for your selected allergens will appear with a black sqaure marked with the words 'may contain' in white, but will not be greyed out.
