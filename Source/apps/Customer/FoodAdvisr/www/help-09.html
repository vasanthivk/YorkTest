You have a number of pre-set profiles to choose from.

"Toggle on the profile you wish to use; only one profile can be used at a time. "

You will see that your selected profile apears in the yellow box at the top, next to 'Profile List'.

Tap this yellow box to go to the details for that profile.

Any excluded items in your selected profile will apear with a red sqaure marked with a white exclamation mark.

You can see which product is excluded by tapping on the sqaure.
